"Iron" Mike Tyson

Mike Tyson in the 80s

This is a tribute page to Mike Tyson's boxing career. Tyson is regarded as one of the most ferocious boxers in history

  • As an amateur, Tyson won gold medals at the 1981 and 1982 Junior Olympic Games, defeating Joe Cortez in 1981 and beating Kelton Brown
  • Tyson made his professional debut as an 18-year-old on March 6, 1985, in Albany, New York. He defeated Hector Mercedes via first-round TKO
  • Tyson became the Undisputed Heavyweight Champion as the culmination of the heavyweight unification series, by unifying his WBA and WBC, with Tony Tucker's IBF belt. The Ring magazine later recognized him as champion on June 27, 1988.
